About Anjali Kaushik Chadha

Seasoned Psychologist & Career Counselor

Anjali Kaushik Chadha is a seasoned psychologist, career counselor, and parenting expert with over 16 years of experience. She has successfully guided more than 100,000+ students, teachers, and parents, both individually and through workshops, across the Delhi-NCR region.

Anjali specializes in addressing mental health concerns, particularly anxiety and depression, through effective psychotherapy techniques. She has helped many individuals overcome these challenges without relying on medication, providing a holistic approach to mental well-being.

She has been a regular contributor to various career and mental health programs, appearing on platforms such as All India Radio FM (AIR FM) and UGC CEC LIVE, where she discussed mental health issues and career opportunities in psychology, agricultural science, biology-based careers, liberal arts, and hotel & hospitality management.

Currently, she serves as a psychologist and career counselor at Bal Bharati Public School in Delhi, where she continues to mentor and guide students on their educational and career paths.
